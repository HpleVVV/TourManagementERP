Tour Agency Pro

Comprehensive Tour & Travel Management Solution for Odoo 18

Core Features

Tour Package Management

Comprehensive tour master data management with categories, itineraries, and media. Keep track of all tour specifications, images, and pricing variants in one place.

Booking & Enrollment

Smart enrollment system with availability tracking and automated confirmations. Manage the entire traveler lifecycle from registration to tour completion.

Passenger Management

Track traveler information, identity documents, and special requirements. Maintain a history of all passengers for personalized future service.

Inquiry & CRM

Dedicated lead pipeline integrated with the website for streamlined conversions. Auto-capture web inquiries and manage them through a professional CRM workflow.

Customer Self-Service Portal

Empower your travelers with a secure portal to view their bookings, download itineraries, and track payment status in real-time.

Hotel & Accommodation

Integrated accommodation database with amenities and room type management. Assign specific hotels to itineraries with precise detail tracking.

Transportation Management

Coordinate fleet and driver assignments for pickups, transfers, and local travel. Ensure smooth logistics for every group and tour.

Meals & Catering

Precise culinary planning within the tour structure. Define meal plans, handle dietary preferences, and coordinate with catering partners across the itinerary.

Sightseeing & Attractions

Points of interest management with entrance ticketing and guide timing. Map out sightseeing activities clearly within the daily itinerary.

Advanced Reporting

Professional PDF itineraries and real-time business performance analytics. Make data-driven decisions with comprehensive dashboards and booking reports.

Advanced Capabilities

Real-time Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards providing insights into booking trends, revenue performance, and tour popularity at a glance.

Financial & Sales Integration

Seamlessly generate sales orders and invoices directly from confirmed bookings. Track your financial health with integrated accounting workflows.

Professional PDF Exports

Export branded, print-ready PDF itineraries that showcase your agency's professionalism and provide travelers with a high-end physical guide.

Multi-Device Compatibility

Optimized for excellence on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Manage your agency from anywhere, at any time.

Dynamic Booking Workflows

Monitor every stage of the tour lifecycle from initial inquiry through to successful completion with custom state transitions.

Automation & Notifications

Leverage automated email templates and notification rules to keep travelers engaged and your team perfectly synchronized.

Technical Information

Installation

  1. Copy the tour_agency folder to your Odoo addons directory
  2. Update the module list in Odoo
  3. Install the module from the Apps menu
  4. Configure your agency settings and start managing your tours
